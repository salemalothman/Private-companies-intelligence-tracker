<objective>
AktaConnector.fetchCompetitors currently returns any company mentioned in industry-filtered
news as a "competitor" — live verification on Canva surfaced NVIDIA, Netflix, and a university
among 16 candidates. Tighten precision with a layered, pure, unit-testable filter that adds
NO new billable calls:

1. Min mention count (>= 2 articles) — single-mention companies are usually incidental.
2. Product-category similarity — resolve surviving candidates via the FREE
   GET /v1/company/search (0 credits) and keep only those whose `product_category` shares a
   substantive token (or crosses a token-Jaccard threshold) with the target's category.
   Unresolvable candidates are dropped; clearly-dead company statuses are dropped (lenient).

Purpose: discovery must return plausibly comparable companies only.
Output: two exported pure functions (`rankIndustryMentions`, `filterRelevantMentions`) with
co-located tests, plus fetchCompetitors orchestration that stays within one /v1/news call.
</objective>
